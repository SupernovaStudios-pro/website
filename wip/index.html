About Supernova Studios

Founded with a passion for creating immersive gaming experiences, Supernova Studios is an independent game development studio dedicated to pushing the boundaries of interactive entertainment.

Our mission is to craft games that not only entertain but also challenge players, creating memorable experiences that stay with them long after they've stopped playing.
